    Bike pooling

    Bike pooling is a practice where people share bikes for traveling purposes. bikes that travel along the same route. Bike pooling has many benefits, such as reducing traffic congestion, lowering pollution, saving costs, and providing real-time navigation.

    Bike Renting

    Bike renting is a service where people can rent bikes for a short or long period of time. Bike renting also provides flexible plans for users, such as hourly, daily, weekly, or monthly rentals.
